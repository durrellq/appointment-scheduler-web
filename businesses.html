Crown & Glory Hair Studio

Dorchester

Beauty

Founded by Maya Johnson in 2015, Crown & Glory specializes in natural hair care, braids, and protective styles with a focus on hair health. Their team of skilled stylists are experts in working with all hair textures and creating stunning styles that celebrate Black beauty.

Soul Cutz Barbershop

Roxbury

Beauty

Marcus Williams established Soul Cutz in 2010 as a community barbershop that offers traditional and modern haircuts, beard trims, and lineup services. The shop has become a cultural hub where clients can enjoy great conversation while getting fresh cuts from skilled barbers.

Legacy Legal Services

Jamaica Plain

Professional

Tiana Roberts founded Legacy Legal in 2018 to provide affordable legal services to small businesses and entrepreneurs. The firm specializes in business formation, contracts, intellectual property, and general business law with a focus on supporting Black-owned businesses.

Taste of Home Restaurant

Mattapan

Food

Chef James Carter opened Taste of Home in 2017, serving authentic Caribbean and Southern cuisine. The restaurant features family recipes passed down through generations, creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where customers can enjoy soul food favorites and island specialties.

Afrocentric Designs

South End

Retail

Founded by Amara Okafor, Afrocentric Designs offers handcrafted clothing, jewelry, and home decor inspired by African cultures. Each piece tells a story and celebrates the rich heritage of the African diaspora, with many items sourced directly from artisans across Africa.

Digital Roots Tech Solutions

Downtown Boston

Tech

Founded by tech entrepreneur Malik Johnson, Digital Roots provides web development, digital marketing, and IT consulting services to small businesses. The company is committed to closing the digital divide by offering affordable technology solutions and free workshops for community members.
