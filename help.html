Getting Started

What is Boston Black Entrepreneurs Appointment Scheduler?

Our appointment scheduling platform helps Black entrepreneurs in Boston streamline bookings and grow their business. It provides an easy way for clients to book appointments online and helps business owners manage their schedule efficiently.

With features like automated reminders, payment processing, and business analytics, our platform is designed to save you time and reduce no-shows, allowing you to focus on what matters most - running your business.

How to Create an Account

  1. Visit our registration page.
  2. Enter your email address and create a password.
  3. Select your account type (Business Owner or Client).
  4. Fill in your personal information.
  5. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox.
  6. Log in to your new account and start setting up your profile!
